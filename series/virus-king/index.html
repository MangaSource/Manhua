Virus King (Doomsday for All: Me, Virus Monarch)

Genre: Apocalypse · Action · Evolution

The world has plunged into the darkness of the apocalypse. In this grim reality, the entire world has become a brutal arena for evolution. It’s humanity’s greatest opportunity and trial, as well as a playground for gods and demons alike. Chen Bai is granted a second chance, going back in time to ten days before the apocalypse began. With a direct injection of the zombie virus, he awakens an SSS-level talent: the Virus King. Now, he embarks on an extraordinary path of evolution, wielding control over the virus itself.
